LIMS / LIS Modern laboratory management

Drive laboratory modernization, efficiency, and productivity

Clinisys is committed to helping you run a data-centric, GenAI-powered laboratory. 

With Clinisys™ Laboratory Solution, you and your organization get a single, configurable SaaS platform that can be configured and optimized for Laboratory Information Management System (LIMS) and Laboratory Information System (LIS) workflows. 

Through adoption and alignment with industry standards and methodologies, preconfigured and standardized workflows, naming conventions and best practices, laboratories can elevate quality and decrease time to value.

What is a LIMS and is it the right information management system for your lab?

A Laboratory Information Management System (LIMS) is sample-centric with a focus on lab workflows for individual and batch test samples with traceability to associated critical data such as instrument calibration and quality controls to support certification. 

What is an LIS and is it the right laboratory information system for your lab?

A Laboratory Information System (LIS) is patient-centric, with the focus on the patient’s personal, demographic, and health data associated with the test for subsequent interpretation and diagnosis.

What is the difference between an LIS and a LIMS?

An LIS is typically limited to medical and healthcare regulations, while a LIMS is focused on standards and regulations for quality, safety, and manufacturing in different industries, including food & beverage, water quality, public health, and environmental.

Why is lab management software important for lab transformation?

Lab management software equips your laboratory and teams with the ability to scale quickly without compromising on quality or regulatory compliance 

With a modern cloud architecture and advanced data model, labs can streamline data management tasks and take advantage of new technologies in a single LIMS interface. This unlocks the flexibility to innovate and support different workloads as labs evolve and grow. 

Our single LIMS and LIS solution

Clinisys™ Laboratory Solution includes a highly configurable SaaS integrated platform for Laboratory Information Management System (LIMS) and Laboratory Information System (LIS) workflows that delivers greater efficiency, scalability, and heightened analytical insights to help create an agile future-proof laboratory.

What are the core capabilities of a LIMS or an LIS?

A modern and powerful laboratory information management system (LIMS) or laboratory information system (LIS) provides essential support to your laboratory irrespective of whether you are focused on patient-centric or sample-centric workflows. There are four universal capabilities: sample management, workflow automation, data management, and quality control and assurance. 
 
As data volumes rise and sample throughput increases, Clinisys™ Laboratory Solution delivers essential software with a modern approach, allowing you to manage, track, and advance workflows with agility and precision. 

Reporting and analytics

Generate standard and custom reports and access tools for data analysis that speed up and strengthen business decision-making.

Streamlined workflow

Enhance and support laboratory workflows, as well as sample tracking across the laboratory, from sample collection to reporting by utilizing automation tools to drive efficiencies through workflow management.

Sample management

Generate and access accurate records for every sample, from the moment it arrives in the lab and throughout its lifecycle.

Data management

Centralise the storage, retrieval, and management of all laboratory data to improve the accuracy and integrity of reports as sample and data volumes increase.

What does the future of lab management look like?

Digitally automated processes and highly flexible systems provide the fundamental foundations required to support the demands of a modern laboratory. 
 
Meanwhile, real-time visibility, cross-discipline collaboration between sites and teams, and seamless access to data and insights help accelerate time to market and elevate the quality and efficiency of services. 
 
With Clinisys™ Laboratory Solution, it’s all possible in a single configurable laboratory information management system.

Why is an effective lab information system so important?

A modern platform approach allows powerful features for sample collection, specimen management, electronic laboratory notebook, instrument management, image and document repository, and compliance.

Clinisys develops common laboratory processes once only, for use across all our customers worldwide. This approach ensures efficiency and resilience. By enabling and deploying processes as required by each customer, we make it easy for new features and capabilities to be developed and deployed in an agile way.

Our data model doesn’t tie us to a patient-centric or sample-centric approach. Instead, we’re able to configure processes in the way that’s best for the needs of the workflow.

What are the benefits of a LIMS or LIS?

A LIMS or LIS provides an essential foundation and is a core asset for any modern laboratory. 

Laboratory software augments the capabilities of your laboratory, maximizes the value of your data, and enhances critical workflows — helping to support your organization’s growth and innovation goals. 

Cost efficiency

Reduce operational costs through automation, and improved efficiency and quality.

Compliance and audit trails

Follow regulatory standards and maintain comprehensive records for audits and reviews.

Scalability

Grow with the laboratory’s needs, accommodating increased data and workflow complexity.

Improved collaboration

Facilitate collaboration between departments and teams and provide easy access to data for authorized users from any location.

Data integrity and quality control

Establish better quality control and assurance processes while maintaining accuracy and consistency of your data across the laboratory.
